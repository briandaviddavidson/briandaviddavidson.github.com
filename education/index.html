Statistics say that every 26 seconds, a child drops out of high school. These students are 3 times more likely to be unemployed than a college graduate. Overall, South Carolina has a 26% dropout rate of high schoolers. Black and Hispanic students are more likely to drop out of high school before completeing their degree. This map displays the dropout rate and the number of students per county by race. More info can be found at boostup.org and dosomething.org
