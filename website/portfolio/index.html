Hootenanny

Hootenanny is an open source conflation tool developed to facilitate automated and semi-automated conflation of critical Foundation GEOINT features in the topographic domain, namely roads (polylines), buildings (polygons), and points-of-interest (POI’s) (points).

FixWikiMaps

As a side project, I created the FixWikiMaps project with some colleagues with the goal in mind to correct, update, and beautify the maps of Wikipedia. The project is live and you can contribute a map or claim one to update.

Current Day Africa

That 70's Show

As an avid fan of the hit series That '70s Show, I really wanted to figure out where the the location of the fictional city of Point Place, WI. I watched the series, recorded the locations, and put it all on a map. Be sure to take a look at the full version.
