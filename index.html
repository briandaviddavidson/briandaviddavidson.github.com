Hello. My name is Brian David Davidson. I'm a recent graduate from the Masters program in the Cartography/GIS program at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I worked with Dr. Robert Roth and my the research was on designing for mobile maps. I completed my undergraduate career at the University of South Carolina under the guidance of Dr. Sarah Battersby and Dr. Michael Hodgson.

Over the past six years, I have worked frequently with JavaScript, jQuery, HTML/CSS, and SQL and have also experience with Java, VB, and COBOL on a variety of different projects throughout my undergraduate and graduate career and have had extensive experience creating, designing, and developing both static and interactive maps, designing vector logos, backgrounds, and icons, and finding data sources. My goal is to help make your cartography and design ideas become a reality. I love to brainstorm and I would love to hear from you so we can discuss your ideas and move forward to make your idea a reality.
