Tourism in Columbia

Welcome to the Tourism in Columbia webpage! Columbia is the capital of the beautiful state of South Carolina and is located in the center of the state. It is about an hour from Charleston, Greeneville, and Charlotte. On this page, you will find some of the many attractions that Columbia has to offer, including many local restaurants, museums, and sport venues! This map only gives you a taste of what Columbia has to offer. For more information on attractions and events that are going on, please check our main webpage that can be found here.
